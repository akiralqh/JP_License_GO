# Privacy Policy for JP License GO

**Last Updated:** March 21, 2026

Welcome to JP License GO ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

### Information We Collect

Our primary goal is to provide you with a great learning experience. The App is designed to function with minimal data collection.

1.  **Locally Stored Data**: The App collects and stores a record of the questions you have answered incorrectly (your "Mistakes"). This information is stored **only on your
device** using standard iOS features (`UserDefaults`). This data is essential for the "Mistake Review" feature to work correctly.

2.  **No Personal Information**: We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, location, or device ID.

### How We Use Your Information

The data stored locally on your device is used solely for the functionality of the App, specifically:

*   To power the "Mistake Review" feature, allowing you to practice questions you got wrong.
*   To track your progress within the app.

This data is never transmitted to us or any third party. It remains on your device and can be cleared by deleting the App.

### Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
