 # Privacy Policy for JP License GO (日本驾照GO)
 
 **Last Updated:** March 31, 2026
 
 Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **JP License GO** ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services.
 
 ### 1. Information the App Collects (Local Storage)
 
 To provide you with an effective learning experience, the App stores certain data locally on your device:
 
 *   **Study Progress & Mistakes**: The App keeps a record of the questions you have answered incorrectly to power the "Mistake Review" feature.
 *   **App Settings**: Your preferred language and other configuration settings.
 
 This data is stored **only on your device** using Apple's standard local storage (UserDefaults). We do not transmit this data to our servers.
 
 ### 2. Information Collected by Third Parties (Advertising)
 
 The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. Google may collect and use certain data to provide personalized ads or measure ad performance. This data may include:
 
 *   **Device Identifiers**: Such as the IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) if you have provided consent via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, or other device-specific IDs.
 *   **Usage Data**: Information about your interactions with advertisements within the App.
 *   **Diagnostic Data**: Crash logs and performance metrics to ensure ad stability.
 
 For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit:
 [Google’s Advertising Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
 
 ### 3. How We Use Your Information
 
 We use the collected information for the following purposes:
 *   **App Functionality**: To track your study progress and provide the "Mistake Review" feature.
 *   **Personalized Advertising**: If consented, Google AdMob uses identifiers to show ads that are more relevant to you.
 *   **Analytics and Improvements**: To monitor app performance and fix bugs.
 
 ### 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
 
 *   We **do not sell** your personal information to third parties.
 *   Data is only shared with third-party service providers (specifically Google AdMob) as described in Section 2.
 
 ### 5. Your Choices and Data Deletion
 
 *   **Tracking Consent**: You can choose whether to allow tracking by the App through the iOS system prompts. You can also change your choice at any time in **Settings > Privacy > Tracking**.
 *   **Data Deletion**: Since all study data is stored locally, you can delete all information collected by the App at any time by **uninstalling the App** from your device.
